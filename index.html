About Us

GTE – Engineering & Technical Solutions

GTE – Groupement Traitement des Eaux – is a specialized engineering company delivering technical and industrial solutions across Algeria. We support professionals and industrial clients in the design, execution, inspection, and compliance of their technical installations. Backed by proven expertise and a highly qualified team, GTE operates with precision and high standards to ensure reliable, efficient, and fully compliant systems.
